About

Igor Morozov is the name behind Hardcore Buddhist projects. Born in 1982 in Ukraine. His music contains such kind of genres as downtempo, psychill, chillgressive, breaks, deep trance, techno and world music elements. It is melodic, dancing and atmospheric, each track is filled with melodic sequences and tight bass lines with either progressive or breaks basis spiced with epic atmospheres and live instruments.
